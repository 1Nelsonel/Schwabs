WE ARE Schwabs Bromedix ltd

Supplier of Hospital Equipment, Laboratory Diagnostics, Laboratory Reagents

Schwabs Bromedix ltd is a fully registered company under the Laws of Kenya companies act(cap 486). It was registered in March 2021 under certicate number PVT-PJUvzLXG. Our company's Head office is situated at Saito centre, Mezzanine door in Eldoret Kenya.

We supply hospital equipment, laboratory diagnostics and reagents to both private and public sectors in Kenya through our dedicated sales personnel and appointed agents.

Our mission focuses on improving the lives of those we serve through providing compassionate care, quality equipment and exceptional customer service. We endeavor to add value to our clients and exceed their expectations with the services we offer.
